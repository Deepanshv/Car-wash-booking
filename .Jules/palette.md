# Palette's Journal - Car Wash Booking App

## 2025-05-14 - Replace native confirm with MUI Dialog
**Learning:** Native `window.confirm` dialogs are jarring and don't match the application's design system, especially in dark mode. MUI Dialogs provide a smoother, more integrated experience and allow for better branding and accessibility.
**Action:** Replace `window.confirm` in `BookingCard` with a styled MUI Dialog that includes the customer's name for better context.
