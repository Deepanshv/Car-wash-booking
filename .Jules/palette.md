## 2026-05-13 - Themed Confirmation Dialogs
**Learning:** Native browser `window.confirm` dialogs feel disjointed from a custom-themed UI and lack support for loading states during asynchronous operations. Replacing them with themed MUI Dialogs improves visual consistency and allows for better user feedback (e.g., loading spinners).
**Action:** Use MUI Dialogs instead of native alerts/confirms to maintain brand identity and provide clear status updates for destructive actions.
