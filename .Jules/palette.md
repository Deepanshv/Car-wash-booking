## 2025-05-14 - Themed Confirmation Dialogs
**Learning:** Native browser dialogs like `window.confirm` break the visual flow of a themed application and offer limited accessibility and feedback options (e.g., no loading states). Replacing them with component-based dialogs (like MUI Dialog) allows for better branding, improved accessibility, and clearer communication of asynchronous processes.
**Action:** Always prefer themed components over native browser alerts/confirms to maintain design consistency and provide better user feedback during destructive actions.
